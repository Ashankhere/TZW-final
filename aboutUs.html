About Us


Team zero waste is a group of students from multidisciplinary backgrounds studying in Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB), aimed towards minimizing the waste generation in the campus and to come up with Solid Waste Management (SWM) solutions. The group is working on developing technical as well as behavioral solutions along with spreading awareness among people for making the IIT Bombay, a zero waste to landfill campus.

Menstrual Waste Awareness Session

The Goal of this session is to spread awareness among people towards menstrual waste and its impacts. We would like to invite young women, eco-conscious people, environmentalists, students, researchers, policymakers, etc., to enlighten ourselves with insights into sustainable menstruation practices and their impact on the circular economy. Our speaker is Chirag. B Virani, Co-founder of Sparkle Innovations, a Surat-based startup by a young couple who aims to make the world a better place by reducing plastic waste and supporting a circular economy. They are producing plastic-free biodegradable pads by utilizing Agro-waste as a resource, which provides extra income to the farmers and reduces air pollution caused by stubble burning.

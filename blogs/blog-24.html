Blog #24




Merry Christmas, 2020



Team Zero Waste wishes you all a very safe and Merry Christmas.

Christmas is to celebrate the birth of Jesus. Don’t make it a reason for death of an innocent. Plastic gifts, X-mas try and decorating items, all these will be discarded and dumped into water bodies or spare land killing animals. Using paper cards will also lead to increase in deforestation. Small steps can make a huge difference in reducing waste and saving the environment. Here are a few steps we can adapt to make Christmas merry for all living beings.

1. Send E-cards instead of paper cards reducing tree killing. In the end your intension and greetings matter.



2. Decorate a real tree instead of a plastic Christmas tree. It will also show your gratitude towards trees which are life givers.



3. Wrap your gifts with newspaper instead of plastic paper. Reusing is the major step we can take to make our lifestyles eco-friendly.



4. Decorate your house with candles and diyas in traditional ways. Don’t use lights which harm the environment and waste electricity both.



In the end, owing to pandemic all over the world, celebrate at home with your family and family.

Stay safe. Stay merry.

Blog #22




Millions of species sharing this Earth



There are millions of species sharing this Earth,

What gave humans the right to exploit others?

Centuries ago a industrial revolution arrived,

It nourished humans but mother nature thrived.

Greenery which gave calmness was replaced,

Fans, coolers and now ACs took its place.

Dirt tracks to the farm on which hide-seek was played,

They are deserted for which video games are blamed.

We are so engrossed from comfort inside at our places,

It is not noticeable that outside we are creating our own graves.

Cutting forests, constructing building and industries,

Has forced wildlife to walk on public streets.

Birds are dying because of more mobile towers than trees,

Global warming is also hiking because of poor environmental strategies.

From water bottle to the pens that we use daily,

Have plastics which are dumped over water bodies carelessly.

It doesn’t get decompose easily for 100 years of lifespan,

Seldomly end up eaten by animals who die of choking and pain.

There is still a little time to think and do some action,

As our future generation needs at least this much protection.

– Shreya Sharma

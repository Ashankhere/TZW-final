Blog #6




Event: Share your story!



Event Name: Share Your Story

Motive: To promote some inspirational stories

Mode of event: Virtual

Description: Nature conservation is one of the biggest problems we are facing nowadays due to mismanagement between people’s lifestyles and nature’s syntax. Therefore, Team Zero Waste presents – “SHARE YOUR STORY” to promote some inspirational stories. If you have any story related to environmental conservation then inspire other people by sharing your story with us. The story will be featured on our Facebook and Instagram pages.

The rule for posting: 1) Write the story in a maximum of 500 words. 2) Send the picture of the person whose story you are sharing 3) You can also use illustrations like posters or videos to convey the story.

Wood Sponges: Recovering Waste Oil

From domestic to industrial use, from automobiles to water-borne vessels, oil is a non-avoidable asset. Oil spills have been every day since exploration times. Leakage occurs on the drilling site due to breakage of the pipeline or sinking of oil tankers. These oil spills cause a significant threat to living organisms, and scientists re working on how to recover oil waste with this new sponge technology technique.
